Spells for Air

NOTE: Some spells will affect you if you do not specify a target.
Test each new spell at each new level.

Lv Spell       Effects
 1 Whip        Attack individual player or monster
 2 Gust        Showoff
 3 Whisper     Send fairy with message to another player
 3 Goodbye     Teleportation, can send someone anywhere
 4 Anchor      Set return point for recall spell (do not anchor in town)
 5 Suffocate   Poison other player, who must buy cure from monk
 6 Twister     Room-wide attack (you will take damage)
 7 Heal        Heals yourself or others
 7 Enchant     Make armor/weapon into Air version, needs harpy feather
 7 Recall      Return to anchor point, if no anchor, works like goodbye
 9 Flameshield Protection from Fire, needs surphur
 9 Aquashield  Protection from Water, needs pearl
10 Gale        Stronger attack than Whip
11 Fury        Room-wide duration attack, lasts in room for a duration of time
12 Disperse    Move ground objects 1 space away from you
13 Breezebend  Curse for weapon, takes away one blessing
13 Starsteel   Blessing for weapon, cast again and again until it fizzles
14 Beastcall   Summon low level Air creature, needs eagle claw
15 Stoneshield Protection from Earth, needs acorn
16 Hurricane   Room-wide attack like twister, but stronger (you will take damage)
17 Windwrap    Blessing for armor, cast again and again until it fizzles
17 Cloudkiss   Curse for armor, takes away one blessing
18 Fiendcall   Summon medium level Air creature, needs eagle claw
19 Vacuum      Stonger version of suffocate
20 Shriek      Highest level attack spell against one opponent
21 Invisible   Make yourself or someone else invisible
22 Scatter     Teleport yourself, others, and ground objects to a random destination
23 Statuette   Makes Air statue, causes low-level Air creature to appear when invoked, needs amethyst
24 Aid         Drains HP and SP to give SP to another player
25 Confusion   Makes another player drunk, wears off, buy cure from monk
26 Demoncall   Summons high level Air creature, needs eagle claw
27 Dustdevil   Makes another player blind
28 Summon      Teleport another player to your location
29 Befuddle    Curse, removes spell levels, buy remove from monk, needs silver feather
30 Aeroslay    Summons level 28 Aerial Servant to kill player or guard room
