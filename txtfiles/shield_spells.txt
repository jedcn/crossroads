Shield Spells

Each element has a shield spell that will increase your
protection.

Each sheild spell must be earned through experience,
and requires a material component to be cast.

Realm Spell Needed Material Component Needed
Air   airshield    harpy feather
Earth stoneshield  acorn
Water aquashield   pearl
Fire  flameshield  sulphur
